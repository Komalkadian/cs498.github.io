Covid Dashboard

About the Dataset: A new coronavirus designated 2019-nCoV was first identified in Wuhan, the capital of China's Hubei province. People developed pneumonia without a clear cause and for which existing vaccines or treatments were not effective. The virus has shown evidence of human-to-human transmission. Transmission rate (rate of infection) appeared to escalate in mid-January 2020. As of 30 January 2020, approximately 8,243 cases had been confirmed. The dataset contains the country wise details of the coronavirus affect, i.e. cases, deaths and recovered per country, mortality rate, which region the county is in etc.

Dataset: COVID 19 Dataset

Visualization Narrative

The Visualization based on the Covid-19 dataset. A detailed view of death rate vs recovered rate per country and how that varied against number of total cases is displayed via three different scenes. Each scene provides an interactive view for the user to access details on demand via tooltip while keeping the visual transition in mind.
